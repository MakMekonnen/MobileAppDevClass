About Me

I am a photographer, 2D animation compositor, and video editor. With photography I aim to use my Fujifilm X-T5 to capture candid moments, cityscapes, and scenes that convey mood and feeling.

With my 2D animation compositing I use After Effects and DaVinci Resolve to create seamless integration between all assets and enhance the emotional impact of each cut.
